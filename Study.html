Study Tips

Let's Get the Work Done

  1. Time Management is key: develop a good schedule that you can keep up with.
  2. Do not procrastinate, you don't want to fall behind.
  3. Put a limit on work hours, you need a break.
  4. Teamwork is necessary: Find study group or partner to keep each other accountable.
  5. Keep your work area clean and organized. Especially when working from home. As work and rest spaces may meld together.
  6. Don't keep doing the same thing over if it doesn't work.
  7. Get to know your learning style and maximize it.
  8. Don't just memorize, understand the concepts and how to apply them.
  9. Embrace the growing pains of learning something new!
