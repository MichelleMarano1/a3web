Here's To Not Burning Out

Tips On Staying Healthy

    1. Hydrate
    Drinking water is one of the most basic necessities to survival, and no matter how busy things get, it is important that we do not neglect our bodies. Staying hydrated will help keep your energy levels throughout the day.
    2. Eat right and snack.
    When learning solely online, it may be tempting to roll out of bed and go to class, however, the age old "Breakfast is the most important meal of the day" saying does not end here. Don't skip meals, eat to replenish your body and your brain.
    3. Exercise
    We all know the health benefits of staying active. But here's a friendly reminder that there is time for work and play.
    4. Prioritize rest: stick to a schedule.
    5. Become a pro napper
    20 minutes throughout the day if you had a late night.
    6. Get fresh air
    Go for a bike ride, a hike, a stroll around your neighbourhood. Clear your mind.
    7. Listen to your body
    8. Find positive classmates to work collaborate with.
    9. Ask for help. Don't be afraid to ask for help!
    10. Set goals for yourself.
    11. Make friends.
