Here's To Not Burning Out

Tips On Staying Healthy

    Taking care of your Body
    1. Hydrate
    2. Eat right and snack: don't skip meals.
    3. Exercise: get moving! Try a standing desk
    4. Prioritize rest: stick to a schedule.
    5. Become a pro napper: 20 minutes throughout the day if you had a late night.
    Taking care of your Mind
    6. Get fresh air: go for a bike ride, a hike, a stroll around your neighbourhood. Clear your mind.
    7. Listen to your body
    8. Find positive classmates to work collaborate with.
    9. Ask for help. Don't be afraid to ask for help!
    10. Set goals for yourself.
    11. Make friends.
